Bioshock Presentation

This project was an extensive presentation comparing System Shock 2, Bioshock, and Bioshock Infinite.
The delivery of the presentation was the main portion but the slides alone still hold a good amount of the message. The presentation was a breakdown of the mechanics and general gameplay differences between the three games.

Overall I was happy with how the entire thing turned out and I felt that I got my point across albeit with a rushed delivery across the ten minute time limit.
