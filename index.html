Hey!
I'm Alex Huard, a Game Designer and Programmer.
This website is to help compile and keep track of all of my projects I have worked on.
I have been making games as a hobby since I was in 5th grade using MMF2 to make games.
As I've continued to make games for almost half my life, I have definitely changed
styles several times and I think the amount of time I have spent working on games has
allowed me to look at my projects with a very critical lens. A lot of my projects end as
prototypes but I think that a good amount of them are interesting ideas that I can learn from.

The majority of my projects now are made in Gamemaker Studio though I dabble with Unity a lot as well.

Right now the main portion of this website is the Games section but I will
eventually add a Tech Demo section where I can organize my little projects that don't become games.

I understand this website looks rough around the edges; I'm a better game designer than a web designer.
